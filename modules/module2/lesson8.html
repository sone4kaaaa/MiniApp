Урок 8: Holidays

New Year - Новый год

New Year is a very special holiday. People celebrate it on the 1st of January. Families decorate their homes with lights and a Christmas tree. Children wait for gifts from Ded Moroz. At midnight, people watch fireworks and say "Happy New Year!". They eat tasty food and enjoy time together. Some people make wishes for the new year.

  • special – особенный [ˈspeʃəl]
  • celebrate – праздновать [ˈseləbreɪt]
  • decorate – украшать [ˈdekəreɪt]
  • gifts – подарки [ɡɪfts]
  • fireworks – фейерверки [ˈfaɪəwɜːks]
  • wishes – желания [ˈwɪʃɪz]

Birthday - День рождения

A birthday is a fun day to celebrate. You get presents and eat cake. Friends and family sing the "Happy Birthday" song. Children often have balloons and games. Some kids have a party at home or a play center. You blow out candles and make a wish.

  • fun – весело [fʌn]
  • presents – подарки [ˈprezənts]
  • song – песня [sɒŋ]
  • balloons – шары [bəˈluːnz]
  • candles – свечи [ˈkændlz]

Halloween - Хэллоуин

Halloween is on the 31st of October. Children wear costumes and go trick-or-treating. They say "Trick or treat!" and get candies. People carve pumpkins into jack-o'-lanterns. It is a little scary, but fun!

  • costumes – костюмы [ˈkɒstjuːmz]
  • trick-or-treating – хеллоуинский обход [ˈtrɪk ɔː ˈtriːtɪŋ]
  • candies – конфеты [ˈkændiz]
  • carve – вырезать [kɑːv]
  • jack-o'-lanterns – светильник из тыквы [ˌdʒæk əʊ ˈlæntənz]

International Women's Day - Международный женский день

On the 8th of March, we celebrate women. Children give flowers to their mothers and teachers. People say kind wishes and smile. It’s a day to say “thank you” to women. There are concerts and cards.

  • wishes – пожелания [ˈwɪʃɪz]
  • smile – улыбаться [smaɪl]
  • concerts – концерты [ˈkɒnsɜːts]
  • cards – открытки [kɑːdz]

Defender of the Fatherland Day - День защитника отечества

On the 23rd of February, we say thank you to boys and men. It is a day for protectors. Boys in school get small gifts. We have games and songs. People say good wishes to grandfathers, fathers, and brothers.

  • protectors – защитники [prəˈtɛktəz]
  • gifts – подарки [ɡɪfts]
  • games – игры [ɡeɪmz]
  • wishes – пожелания [ˈwɪʃɪz]

Knowledge Day - День Знаний

The 1st of September is the Day of Knowledge. It is the first day of school. Children wear uniforms and bring flowers to their teachers. There is a bell ceremony. Teachers and students are happy to see each other again.

  • school – школа [skuːl]
  • flowers – цветы [ˈflaʊəz]
  • bell – звонок [bel]
